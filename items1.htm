Screen by screen descriptions > Task Maintenance

Items

This tab shows details of all the items attached to the task – including the sheet (print change), number up, quantity (this is the good quantity out of the step so does not include make ready or running waste). Depending on the Radius system linked to the dimensions may or may not be shown.
Clicking on the item line displays full item details including a long description, delivery address and again depending on the Radius system the first call off date and delivery date.

On the left the sheet numbers can be resequenced. The new sequence will appear in the grid above and on work to lists so that production is in the correct order.

Below an area shows item approval status for those using the linked approval system
