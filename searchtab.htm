Search tab

This tab has numerous filters to enable the required orders to be found. If not visible move the mouse over the green bar on the left. It will stay open until the Filter button is pressed

 Especially important is the ‘Orders deleted from Radius’ filter that should be used if a warning about such orders is received on opening or after update and the reason determined. Either the status in Radius must be corrected or the order also deleted from PSA.
By clicking in the title bar of the table the results may be sorted by the selected column.
Column widths may be adjusted in the usual manner.
