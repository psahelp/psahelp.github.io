What-If mode
This is how all previous versions of PSA worked for all users.
On opening a schedule the entire database is copied either to your local PC or your local work area in the case of Citrix/Terminal Server users.
A second local copy is immediately taken for reference purposes.
All changes are made in, and details displayed from, the first local copy.
When updating the plan, PSA compares the changed and unchanged local copies to determine, at field level, what has been changed.
These field level changes are applied to the central schedule database.
The cycle then starts again with two full copies being taken of the central schedule making available any changes applied in the meantime by other users or transfers from Radius.
