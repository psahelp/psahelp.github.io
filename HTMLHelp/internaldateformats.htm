Internal Date Formats

PSA and SFDC use historic internal date formats to indicate start and end times of tasks
In the case of SFDC this ‘number of seconds since 1/1/1990’
In the case of PSA this ‘number of minutes since 1/1/1995’

To convert PSA fields job.strtime and job.edtime to date/time format paste the value into cell A1 of a spreadsheet and put the formula =A1/1440+34700 into cell B1
Where 1440 is the number of minutes in a day and 34700 is the number of days between 1/1/1900 and 1/1/1995
If the value in A1 is 11182345 then the value in B1 will be 42465.51736
Formatted as a Date/Time – dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm this will display as 05/04/2016 12:25
To convert a date/time into PSA format subtract 34700 and multiply by 1440
