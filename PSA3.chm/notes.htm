Notes

Up to 30 semi structured notes may be added to each order and appear on this tab. They can also appear on the planning board and could be incorporated into reports if required.
The titles are defined under Configuration/Tables to the left of the Technical Attributes tab. By giving them defined titles they can be used for temporary information during the course of the order through the plant such as ‘Plate, Ink or Tool Locations’, ‘Additional material details’ etc.
