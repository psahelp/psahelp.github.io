Materials

Material details are imported from Radius with one of six statuses - 1 - not actioned - 2 - ordered - 3 - available - 4 – issued – 5 – to be called off and – 6 – called off. However it is not just the status but the status in relation to the required date that is important in PSA and this is where it is all configured.

Having imported material groups via PsaConnect it is important to mark those that are ‘critical’ here. This is where Radius will be supplying expected delivery dates via the Materials Requirement system or the availability is regularly reassessed via Free Stock Planning routines. If such details are available mark it critical – otherwise leave it non critical.
Material availability is accessed separately to resource availability.
It is recommended to use the ‘Restore’ button to divide all possible combinations (6 status values x 2 critical path values x 3 combinations of available date v required date - a total of 36) into coloured groups depending on whether the material is not critical, expected in time, expected late or some other action is required.

To view the results on the planning board open the resource pop up and select only the material line - always the second option.

By clicking on the labels to the right you can change both the colour and text associated with each colour

Select one of the rows allows you to adjust any combination of critical/status/dates to any band. After changing any bands you should run a full assessment check to reflect the new bands.
