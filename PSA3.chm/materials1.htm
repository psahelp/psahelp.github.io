Materials

This screen shows details of materials used in the order. Materials are attached to the task where they are used. When linked to a Radius I system packing materials are not attached to any task. However, in PSA they are attached – either to the first task of the relevant component or a task on a work centre that is defined as using packing material.

Materials may be added, deleted and modified in the same way as tasks except that details are changed in the panel below the list. Normally the details are passed from Radius by PsaConnect. The availability status will be updated automatically as Radius is amended to handle the changes.

This screen now shows the Material Code with which users are most familiar from Radius. Column widths are adjustable and remembered so that unused columns may be hidden. When a row is selected the detail appears below.

The status field has one of six values which correspond approximately to. 1) No action taken. 2) Material on order. 3) Material in stock. 4) Material issued to the job. 5) Ordered but still to be called off. 6) Ordered and called off.

Required date - is the scheduled start date of the linked task.
Available date – is the expected delivery date for which the material has been ordered
Confirmed date – is the date the supplier has confirmed delivery will be made
Critical – all materials should be divided into Critical and not Critical based on material group and sub group (type). Those marked Critical will have separate requirements in Radius and subsequent updates of status.
The colour shown behind the Cmp column is determined by a combination of critical, status, and how required date is related to available or confirmed date. See under Configuration/System/Materials for the setting of these colours. The actual colour will imply whether all is OK or action is required.

If using Free Stock Planning then the ‘Confirmed Date’ for a PO will not be displayed but the Available date will reflect the confirmed date
