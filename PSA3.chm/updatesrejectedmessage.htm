Updates rejected message

Occasionally after updating you may see a message of the form ‘All updated to jobs ‘12345’, ‘23456’ etc have been rejected because of recent changes from Radius’.

This occurs under two circumstances.

First – the job was not scheduled when you opened the plan and in the time between your open/last sync and your update the job has been reconfirmed in Radius and transferred by PsaConnect. This means that the new job in the central SQL db is no longer the same job as the one you have been scheduling/changing. It is therefore not valid to apply your changes.

Second – the job was scheduled but again, in the time between your open/last sync the job you have changed has been replaced by another user merging a replacement from Radius. So again the job you are trying to update no longer exists in the central SQL db and updates to it are rejected

To avoid the problem increase the frequency of updates – at a busy plant updates should not be delayed more than 20 or 30 minutes
