Schedule Control

Schedule Control allows the organisation of all unplanned, planned and scheduled orders.

Replacement orders will always appear on the unplanned tab unless they have been auto merged during the update process.

Orders on the planned tab should generally be sorted into the required production sequence with the most urgent at the top where they remain visible on entry.
