Parameters tab - Calendar area

This section determines how week numbers are calculated and how the calendar will display

Week begins on - normally Monday in Europe and Australasia (ISO 8601), Sunday in the Americas, China and South Africa and Saturday in the middle East.

Week 1 selects the rule determining the first week of the year. There are 3 options.


1) 1st January is always in week 1. This means week 1 starts between 26th December and 1st January. This is the norm outside Europe and Australasia


2) Week 1 has at least 4 days in the New Year – (ISO 8601) – thus the earliest it can start is 29th December and the latest is 4th January.


3) Week 1 is the first full week in the New Year – it will therefore start between the 1st and 7th January.


Tick the check box if you wish week numbers to display on all calendars. The calendar will also display the week days starting with the selected day.
