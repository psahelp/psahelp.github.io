Other Reports

·      Work in Progress – lists quantity completed against task quantity for completed tasks of selected process types

·      Daily Machine Program – Customer specific report – requires fixed display code X_Bind and X_Press to enable

·      Daily Running List – fixed format report listing just job numbers in one hour time slots

·      Production Master Plan – provides a user definable selection of attributes (selection can be named and saved – because of large selection output is in very small format

There is also summary function split into Provisional and Confirmed jobs

·      Material Call Off – list of material requirements by supplier – where information is available

·      Top 50 – customer specific

·      Proof and Process – customer specific – links to Radius II tables so requires a suitable DSN to be set up

·      Make Ready Reduction – where automatic make ready reduction has been set allows a report of savings to be listed – reductions can be negative where minimum make ready applies

·      KeyDateCus –specialised – unlikely to be of interest to most users

·      KeydateLoc –specialised – unlikely to be of interest to most users

·      Workitems – customer specific – unlikely to be of interest to most users

·      Worksinstr – customer specific – unlikely to be of interest to most users
